About POSmart

POSmart, or 'Point of Sale System Mart,' is a user-friendly web application built for People's Mart vendors. It streamlines transaction processes, reduces errors, and saves time by automating point-of-sale and inventory management, eliminating manual paper-based record-keeping.

WHAT IS PEOPLE'S MART?

People’s Mart is a large public market located in Liboton, Naga City. It plays a significant role in the city's economy and is a popular spot for locals to shop for a variety of goods.

POSmart aims to help these small businesses, especially vendors that do not have any access to technology like People’s Mart. And now, thanks to POSmart, these vendors can focus on what they do best—serving their customers—while letting POSmart handle the rest.

SERVICES

In POSmart, we provide our customers with two services: the Point Of Sale System and the Inventory Management System.

Point Of Sale System

Vendors at People's Mart no longer need to manually write down order details on the paper. They can input product information like name, description, quantity, price, category, etc. into our system, which instantly generates a digital receipt for each order.

Inventory Management System

 Our system empowers People's Mart vendors to effortlessly monitor their product inventory in real-time. They can add, modify, delete, and update product information to simplify inventory management.
